- othello game
- How to play:
	1) The player must place disk adjacent to the opponents disk so that the opponent's disk
	is sandwiched between the two dics in which the oppoenent's discs will be flipped
	2) Available moves are marked with a dot '.'
	3) A disc is only flipped when it is the direct result of a move
	4) If the player has no available moves (cannot flip opponent's disk), then they must 
	forfeit their turn to the other player
	5) The player with the most discs at the end wins
